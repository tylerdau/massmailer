                <p>It was wonderful seeing so many of you at the meeting last week! What a great meeting it was, and <b>so much thanks to Dr. Juliet Sekandi</b> for her exciting, enriching discussion <b>on the dual epidemic of HIV and TB in Africa!</b></p>

                <p>Dr. Sekandi is putting together a project to use Mobile Technology to benefit Women's Health in Uganda, and would love you to be involved. <a href="mailto:bbeisel@medlifeweb.org"><font color="C14C43">Email Brock Beisel (bbeisel@medlifeweb.org)</font></a> to learn more and become involved!</p>

                <p>Due to the critically low turnout at the Fall Concert last week, we truly want to hear feedback to help us improve. <i>If you were not at the concert</i>, please take 2 minutes and honestly <a href="http://goo.gl/forms/j9GvnJukwx"><font color="C14C43">fill out this short feedback form.</a></font> All responses are anonymous, we just are trying to learn how to improve turnout.</p>

                <p>These large fundraisers are vital to the success of our organization, so it is imperative that we improve attendance and you make every effort to be at these big events - we will do out best to make it better and easier for you. Even if you couldn’t make it out to our concert, <b>please consider donating a ticket’s price to the Bathroom Project fund by donating to MEDLIFE at UGA.</b> <a href="https://www.paypal.com/cgi-bin/webscr?cmd=_s-xclick&hosted_button_id=264NDFQXCM57W"><font color="#C14C43">Donate a ticket's price here.</font></a></p>

                <p>This coming weekend <b>we really need your help</b> at the 3rd <a href="http://goo.gl/forms/3IRT06WHsy"><font color="C14C43">Sanford Stadium Cleanup</font></a>! This is an amazing opportunity for us to contribute to the eco-efforts of our community and earn funds for our mission! <b>We earn $500 if 31+ people show, so please come out!</b></p>

                <p>A great social opportunity is coming this Thursday, 5 November 2015 from 7pm to 9pm with the <b>Fall 2015 Game Night!</b> This epic evening will include Settlers of Catan, Monopoly, Cards, and more, and will be at <a href="https://goo.gl/maps/CgHxKqVBF2J2"><font color="C14C43">Apt 3, 210 East Cloverhurst Avenue, Athens, GA 30605.</font></a></p>
                <p>This is just a fun opportunity to <b>get to know other MEDLIFE Members, Exec, and Family Heads, so please make an effort to come out!</b>

                <p><b>On the service front</b>, here are some ways we would love you involved over the  next few weeks! <a href="http://goo.gl/forms/oz3IOug6Dj"><font color="C14C43">Pruitt Health Grandview Walk and Talk</font></a>, <a href="http://goo.gl/forms/3IRT06WHsy"><font color="C14C43">Sanford Stadium Cleanup</font></a>, <a href="http://goo.gl/forms/ZctJLuS4cW"><font color="C14C43">Project Safe</font></a>, <a href="http://goo.gl/forms/2wno1BMtXb"><font color="C14C43">Foodbank</font></a>, <a href="http://goo.gl/forms/HEk7P05l21"><font color="C14C43">UOWN: Oconee Water Quality Sampling</font></a>, <a href="http://goo.gl/forms/rjka8AnXW8"><font color="C14C43">Teach Health and Nutrition</font></a>, and <a href="http://goo.gl/forms/dPD267oFQq"><font color="C14C43">Blood Drive</font></a> are all great opportunities; we cannot wait to see you there!</p>

                <p>We are also supporting the Turkeypalooza canned food drive by <u>gathering donations of cream of mushroom soup and pumpkin puree!</u> <b>Our goals is 50 cans and the drive runs through 19 November 2015.</b> Please <u>bring cans to the donation box in our cubicle (Cubicle 16 in the CSO)!</u></p>

                <p>Finally, <b>if you are interested in traveling abroad with MEDLIFE on a Mobile Clinic</b>, be sure to attend the Mobile Clinics Interest Meeting following our General Body Meeting next week! That's 8:30pm on 10 November 2015 in MLC 102, immediately following our General Body meeting.</p>

                <p>We cannot wait to see you out there, so please <b>sign-up below</b> for all of these superb opportunites!</p>
